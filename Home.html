Applicaton of Computers

Computers have transformed the banking industry, making it more efficient, convenient, and secure for customers and allowing banks to offer a wide range of services.

Computers have made it possible to handle large volumes of transactions quickly and accurately.  They made it possible to automate many processes that were once done manually, like account opening, deposit processing, loan approvals, and various transactions.
