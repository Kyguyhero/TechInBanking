Positives

Credit Cards

Thanks to computers, credit cards become very popoular among the general public due to their accessibility and convenience, enabling everyone to shop and manage their money without carrying around larges amounts of cash.

Credit cards provide a useful record of purchasing history, making it easier for consumers to manage their finances and track their purchases.

Credit cards can also offer fraud protection, reducing the risk of financial loss due to unauthorized, unwanted transactions.

Not to mention, credit cards have played a significant role in stimulating consumer spending and driving economic growth, due to the fact that people can make purchases beyond their immediate financial means.

Online Banking

Online banking has made it easy for individuals to perform various banking activities, such as checking account balances, transferring funds, paying bills, and applying for loans, all from the comfort of their homes using computers.

Banks can provide real-time updates and notifications for important financial decisions, which reduces the likelihood of errors and other discrepancies.

With online banking, there is no need to visit physical banks, which saves customers time and money. It also eliminates the need for physical cash and checks.

Mobile Banking

Similar to online banking, mobile banking has made accessing finances incredibly easy. Banks make it simple for users to use apps on their phones or tablets to manage their accounts.

Mobile banking apps have enhanced secruity features that protect user data. This includes features like fingerprint and facial recognition, secure login credentials, and encryption protocols.

Regulations

The online banking world has been subject to stringent regulations and restrictions to ensure the security and privacy of customers' financial information, including encryption, verification services, and regular audits.

Online banks impose strict rules on online banking institutions to prevent money laundering, fraud, and other illicit activities. Banks will also use transaction monitoring systems to detect and report suspicious transactions.

Business

Online banking has streamlined financial transactions and reduced administrative overheads, saving businesses' time and money.

Businesses get access to real-time financial information, allowing them to make informed decisions and improve financial management.

Online banking has expanded businesses' reach by enabling secure and convenient online payment options, facilitating global transactions and opening doors to new markets.
